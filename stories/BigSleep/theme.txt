The theme of this story is the corrupting influence of power. The victim was a politician, and the suspects are all people with influence and money. The detectives only ally is the beautiful client, but even she may be hiding something. As the investigation progresses, the detective realizes that everyone involved is willing to lie, cheat, and even kill to protect their secrets. The murderer is also powerful and is closing in on the detective. In the end, the theme is about how even good people can be corrupted by power.
