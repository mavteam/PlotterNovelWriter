What is the dark side of the following universal truth? Write an essay on the topic.

UNIVERSAL TRUTH: The universal truth in this story is that no matter how difficult things get, you should never give up hope. Things may seem hopeless, but there is always a way to persevere.

DARK SIDE ESSAY:

==========

The dark side of this universal truth is that sometimes things really are hopeless. No matter how much you hope and pray, sometimes there is just no way to make things better. This can be incredibly difficult to accept, but it is important to remember that hope is not always enough. Sometimes, the only thing you can do is accept the situation and move on.
