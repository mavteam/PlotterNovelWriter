NEXT CHAPTER: .Captain J.T. Wilder of the starship USS Ernest Rutherford is on a five-year mission to explore deep space.

SCENE 1:

==========

Captain Wilder is on the bridge of the starship. He is discussing the mission with his first officer, Commander James Smith.

SCENE 2: Captain Wilder is in the mess hall of the starship eating dinner with Commander Smith and Lt. Commander Michael Jones, the ship’s second officer.

SCENE 3: Captain Wilder is in his quarters on the starship reading a book when he gets a call from Admiral Miller, who commands Star Fleet Command. Miller tells Wilder that there has been an attack on Star Fleet Headquarters by an unknown enemy and that they are now at war with an alien race called the Ssaurians. Miller orders Wilder to take his ship to Mars and join up with other Star Fleet ships there to get ready for battle.

SCENE 4: The starship arrives at Mars and joins up with other Star Fleet ships that have also been ordered there by Admiral Miller. The ships are all being commanded by their captains because Admiral Miller has been killed in the attack on Star Fleet Headquarters by the Ssaurians. They await further orders from Admiral M’Benga, who has taken over command of Star Fleet until a new admiral can be appointed by President Henderson of Earth.
