What is the dark side of the following universal truth? Write an essay on the topic.

UNIVERSAL TRUTH: The universal truth in this story is that no matter how difficult things get, you should never give up hope. Things may seem hopeless, but there is always a way to persevere.

DARK SIDE ESSAY:

==========

The dark side of this universal truth is that sometimes things really are hopeless. No matter how much you hope and try, there are just some things that cannot be overcome. This can be incredibly frustrating and discouraging, leading you to give up entirely. It's important to remember, though, that even in these situations, it's never wise to give up hope entirely. There may not be a way to fix the problem, but there is always a way to find peace and acceptance.
