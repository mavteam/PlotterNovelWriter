Write a concise summary of the following story.


Captain Wilder and Ensign O'Neil discover a wormhole while scanning the outer planets of the Alpha Centauri system. They take the Ernest Rutherford through the wormhole and find themselves in the middle of a battle between an alien ship and a ship from a distant galaxy. The aliens shoot the Ernest Rutherford's engines, causing the ship to crash-land on a nearby planet. The crash leaves the Ernest Rutherford a total loss.  Captain Wilder, Ensign O'Neil, and the rest of the crew manage to get to the damaged ship and get it ready to leave. Ensign O'Neil takes a shuttle to the alien ship and manages to open the hangar bay doors so that the Ernest Rutherford can fly out of the alien ship. Back on the Ernest Rutherford, they realize they have plenty of power, but they were unable to repair the engines. Ensign O'Neil takes a second shuttle and opens the hangar bay doors on the other side of the ship. The Ernest Rutherford flies out of the alien ship and heads towards the wormhole.
