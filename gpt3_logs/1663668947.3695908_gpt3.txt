What is the dark side of the following universal truth? Write an essay on the topic.

UNIVERSAL TRUTH: The universal truth in this story is that no matter how difficult things get, you should never give up hope. Things may seem hopeless, but there is always a way to persevere.

DARK SIDE ESSAY:

==========

The dark side of this universal truth is that sometimes people do give up hope. They may feel like they've tried everything and there is just no way out. This can lead to depression, suicide, and a whole host of other problems. It's important to remember that there is always hope, no matter how dark things seem.
