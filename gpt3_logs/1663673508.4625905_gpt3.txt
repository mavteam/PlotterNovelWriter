Write a concise summary of the following story.


Captain Wilder and Ensign O'Neil find a wormhole while exploring the Alpha Centauri system. They enter the wormhole and find themselves in the middle of a battle between two ships from different galaxies. The aliens shoot the Ernest Rutherford's engines, causing the ship to crash-land on a nearby planet. The crew of the Ernest Rutherford manage to get the ship ready to leave, but they are attacked by aliens and crash land again. Trapped on the ship, the crew figures out a way to defeat the aliens and escape. Wilder is taken prisoner by the aliens and taken to their home world. He is injected with a drug and taken to a medical facility where the aliens examine him. Wilder is taken back to his cell, but he escapes and makes his way back to the Ernest Rutherford. He takes off in the shuttle and heads back towards the wormhole.  Aliens remove Wilder's eyes and replace them with strange orbs. Wilder wakes up from the operation and learns that the aliens are telepaths. He can hear them and they can hear him. Wilder is returned to his cell. Wilder convinces the aliens to let him go and leave. The aliens let him go and he returns to the Ernest Rutherford. The crew get ready to leave, but the aliens attack and Wilder is taken prisoner.
