What is the light side of the following universal truth? Write an essay on the topic.

UNIVERSAL TRUTH: The universal truth in this story is that no matter how difficult things get, you should never give up hope. Things may seem hopeless, but there is always a way to persevere.

LIGHT SIDE ESSAY:

==========

No matter how difficult things get in life, it is important to never give up hope. Things may seem hopeless at times, but there is always a way to persevere. When you keep hope alive, you give yourself the chance to overcome any obstacle. Hope is what gives us the strength to keep going, even when everything seems against us. It is what gives us the courage to face our fears and the determination to reach our goals. So never give up hope, because it is the light that will guide you through the darkness.
