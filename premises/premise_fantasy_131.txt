Dragonsworn follows the story of half-dragon, half-human protagonist as they quest to find a way to break the ancient magical Dragon Blood oath that compels them to serve a terrible and tyrannical Emperor. Along the way, they make allies and enemies among the other downcast creatures of the empire, including shifters, ghosts, and vampires. Blood is spilled, secrets are uncovered, and the world teeters on the brink of catastrophe. it's only through the power of the protagonist's bond with their dragon companion that they might be able to save the day... if it's not too late.
