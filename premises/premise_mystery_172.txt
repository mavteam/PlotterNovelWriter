After the death of her husband, Donna moves to the small town of Rocksville to start over. She opens a bookstore and starts to get to know her neighbors. One night, she finds one of her neighbors, Bill, dead in his home. She calls the police and starts to snoop around to try to find out who killed him. She quickly discovers that almost everyone in the town had a reason to hate Bill. As she starts to eliminate suspects, she gets closer and closer to the truth. But when the killer starts to pay attention to her snooping, she realizes that she might be in danger. Can she figure out who killed Bill before it's too late?
