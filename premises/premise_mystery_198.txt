In the late hours of a rainy night, a young woman is found brutally murdered in an alleyway. Detective Makoto Watanabe is called to the scene, and quickly begins his investigation. Every lead seems to dead end, until Makoto stumbles upon a vital clue: a piece of paper with a list of names and numbers, all of them belonging to influential people in the city. As Makoto begins to piece together the victim's final days, he realizes that she was caught up in a web of lies, betrayal, and corruption. Every step closer to the truth only seems to make the case more complicated, but Makoto won't stop until he finds the killer. With the help of his partner, Detective Nobue Suzuki, they'll track down every lead, no matter how dangerous it may be.
