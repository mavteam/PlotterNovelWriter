In a noir anime mystery story, a detective is investigating a string of murders. The victims all seem to be connected to a powerful politician. The detective's only clue is a mysterious blue rose that keeps turning up at the crime scenes. As the detective delves deeper into the case, he discovers that the politician is part of a secret society that is involved in some very shady business. The society is also responsible for the death of the detective's partner. The detective ultimately faces off against the society in a final showdown. He manages to expose their crimes to the public and bring the members to justice.
