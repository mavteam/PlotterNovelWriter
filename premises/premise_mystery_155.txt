Widow Maggie Bradley moves to the small town of Shepherdstown to start fresh after her husband's untimely death. She quickly befriends her next door neighbor, Betty, and joins the local knitting club. One day, while out on a leisurely walk, Maggie stumbles across the dead body of one of the club members, Ruth. Maggie quickly becomes the prime suspect in the investigation and must use all her yarn-crafting knowledge to clear her name and catch the real killer.
