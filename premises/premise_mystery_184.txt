In a dark and seedy city, the body of a young woman is found charred beyond recognition. The only clues to her identity are a mysterious tattoo on her left arm and a single black button sewn into the lining of her coat. Detective Ayaka Kurenai is tasked with solving the case. She quickly discovers that the victim was a member of a powerful and influential crime family. She also finds evidence linking the death to a string of arson cases across the city. As she delves deeper into the case, Kurenai uncovers a complex web of lies and deceit. She eventually comes to realize that the victim was far from the innocent victim she first appeared to be. The case leads Kurenai down a dark and dangerous path, one that threatens to consume her completely. But she's determined to see it through to the end, even if it means sacrificing everything she holds dear.
