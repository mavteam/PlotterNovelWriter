When her father dies unexpectedly, Shannon is left to run the family farm on her own. She quickly discovers that she is in over her head and the farm is in danger of being foreclosed on. Shannon decides to take a risk and hire a team of migrant workers to help her save the farm. The workers are hard-working and Shannon soon finds herself developing feelings for one of them, Diego. However, she knows that it would be inappropriate to pursue a relationship with someone she is employer. Shannon and Diego must find a way to resist their feelings for each other and focus on saving the farm. As the harvest season approaches, Shannon and her team are in a race against the clock to get the crops in. At the same time, Shannon is struggling to keep her feelings for Diego in check. With everything on the line, Shannon has to decide what is more important: her relationship with Diego or the farm.
