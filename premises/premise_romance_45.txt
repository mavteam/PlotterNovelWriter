Allison is a young woman who has just moved to a small town in Colorado. She's not expecting much from her new life, but she's hoping to find a new start. When she meets Luke, a handsome stranger who seems to know a lot about her, she's immediately drawn to him. Luke is actually a werewolf, and he's been watching Allison from afar for some time. He finally decides to approach her when he realizes that she's in danger from a group of vampires who are targeting her for her unique abilities. Allison is initially scared of Luke, but she ultimately decides to trust him. Together, they must protect Allison from the vampires and figure out what her unique abilities are. Along the way, they fall in love with each other, and Allison learns that the supernatural world is far more complicated than she ever could have imagined.
