Delia is a small-town librarian who loves nothing more than spending her days surrounded by books. So when she stumbles upon a dead body in the library, she takes it upon herself to solve the mystery. With the help of her trusty cat, Sherlock, Delia sets out to discover who killed the victim and why. Along the way, she uncovers a conspiracy that goes all the way to the top of the local government. With time running out, can Delia solve the mystery before the killer claims another victim?
