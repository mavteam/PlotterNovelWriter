After a powerful storm destroys the moon, the Earth is left in complete darkness. inhabitants are left to fend for themselves against roving gangs of criminals. Desperately seeking a new source of energy, a team of scientists journey to the sun in alast-ditch effort to save the planet. But when they arrive, they find that the sun has mysteriously disappeared. With the Earth's resources rapidly dwindling, the scientists must find a way to reactivate the sun before it's too late.
