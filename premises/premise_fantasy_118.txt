The high fantasy story follows the story of young woman who discovers she has the ability to control dragons. With this power, she must help save her kingdom from an evil king who wishes to enslave all the dragons. Along the way, she meets many allies, including a wise old woman who helps her to understand her powers, and a handsome prince who may have his own hidden agenda. Together, they must overcome great obstacles, including giant dragons, treacherous mountains, and an army of the king's best soldiers. In the end, they triumph and the kingdom is saved.
