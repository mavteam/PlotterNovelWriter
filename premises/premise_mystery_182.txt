When the body of a young woman is found dead in an alleyway, Detective Shinya Kazama is called in to investigate. It quickly becomes clear that this is no ordinary murder, and Kazama will have to put all his skills to the test to catch the killer. With the help of his partner, Detective Ayumu Mikami, they'll piece together the evidence and solve the case. But this is just the beginning, as they soon discover that the murder is only the tip of the iceberg in a much larger conspiracy. With corruption and danger lurking around every corner, Kazama and Mikami will have to be careful not to get in too deep as they race against the clock to catch the killer before it's too late.
