Satoshi Noguchi is a private investigator with a dark past. When he was just a child, his parents were brutally murdered in front of him by a unknown assailant. This event traumatized Noguchi and left him with a burning desire to find out who killed his parents and why. Noguchi's search for answers leads him down a dark path of crime and violence. He quickly discovers that his parents' murder is just one small piece of a much larger puzzle. Slowly but surely, Noguchi starts to piece together the truth, but the closer he gets to the truth, the more dangerous it becomes. With time running out, Noguchi must put everything on the line to find the answers he's been looking for his entire life. But will the truth be worth the price he has to pay?
