In a land of eternal night, ruled by a corrupt aristocracy, a group of rebels rises up to overthrow the government. Their leader is a skilled warrior, who was once the king's champion. But when the king betrayed him, he turned to a life of crime. Now, he will stop at nothing to get his revenge. Hunted by the king's forces, the rebels must take refuge in the dark forests and caves of the land. There, they will face untold dangers, from giant spiders to bloodthirsty vampires. But the stakes are high, and failure is not an option. With the help of his rebel allies, the warrior will stop at nothing to take down the king and restore justice to the land.
