Aspiring actress Maya has just arrived in Mumbai to make it big in Bollywood. She meets handsome businessman Rohan at a party and they immediately hit it off. Rohan offers to help Maya get her foot in the door in the film industry, and the two begin working together. As they spend more time together, they begin to develop feelings for each other. However, Rohan is already in a relationship with another woman and is hesitant to pursue anything with Maya. Maya is heartbroken but doesn't want to give up on her dreams, so she throws herself into her work. Things come to a head when Rohan's girlfriend finds out about his feelings for Maya and breaks up with him. Rohan finally admits his feelings for Maya and they begin dating. However, the path to true love is never easy and the two face many challenges along the way. Can their relationship survive the pressures of the film industry?
