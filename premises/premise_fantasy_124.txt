When 18-year-old Arionna is taken from her home in the middle of the night by a group of strange men, she has no idea what's in store for her. She's been chosen to take part in a sacred ritual that will grant her the power to commune with the spirit world. With the help of her newly appointed spirit guide, Arionna will embark on a journey to save her people from a dark force that threatens to destroy them. Along the way, she will form unlikely alliances and discover hidden strengths she never knew she had.
