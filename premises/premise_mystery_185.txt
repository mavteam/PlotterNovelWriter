When Kiryu, a small-time private investigator, is hired to find a missing person, he has no idea what he's getting himself into. The missing person turns out to be a young woman named Ai, who is involved in a much larger conspiracy than Kiryu could have imagined. Ai's disappearance is just the tip of the iceberg, and as Kiryu delves deeper into her case, he uncovers a web ofcrime and corruption that reaches into the highest levels of the city's power structure. With enemies on all sides and time running out, Kiryu will have to use all of his detective skills to solve the case and save Ai's life.
