When the world was young, the gods created mortals to rule over it. However, the gods grew tired of the mortals' constant fighting and decided to withdraw their support. The mortals were left to fend for themselves, and over time they forgot about the gods. Without the gods' guidance, the mortals descended into chaos. The strong emerged victorious, while the weak were left to suffer. In this dark age, a hero arises. This hero is blessed by the gods and is tasked with defeating the evil that has taken hold of the world. The hero gathers a group of brave souls and sets out on a journey to vanquish the evil. Along the way, they face many challenges and make new allies. Finally, they reach their destination and face the evil head-on. In a titanic battle, the hero emerges victorious and peace is restored to the world.
