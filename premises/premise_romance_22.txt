A young woman, Sarah, falls in love with a dashing young Frenchman, Mathieu. They have a whirlwind romance, meeting in secret and spending every moment they can together. However, their families are against the relationship and try to keep them apart. Mathieu is eventually sent away to study in Paris and Sarah is left behind, heartbroken. They keep in touch through letters and eventually decide to run away together. However, on the night they are supposed to meet, Sarah's father forbids her from leaving and Mathieu is forced to go without her. They both marry other people but are never truly happy, always thinking of what might have been. They meet again years later, now both widowed, and finally have their happy ending together.
