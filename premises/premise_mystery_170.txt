Catamountain Lodge is a cozy little bed and breakfast situated in the mountains outside of Aspen, Colorado. When the owner, Mrs. Danvers, is found dead in her own bed, it's up to her niece, Darcy, to solve the mystery. With the help of her trusty feline friend, Sherlock, Darcy sets out to discover who killed her aunt and why. Along the way, she uncovers secrets, scandal, and more than one motive for murder. Ultimately, it will take all of her sleuthing skills to solve this puzzle and catch the killer.
