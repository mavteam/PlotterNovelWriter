A young woman named Aisha is betrayed by her lover, Dev. Dev was Aisha's first and only love, and she is heartbroken when she finds out that he was only using her for her money. Aisha's best friend, Priya, helps her get through the heartbreak and Dev is soon out of her life. Aisha meets Raj, a wealthy man who is also a loner. Raj is immediately attracted to Aisha and they begin a passionate affair. Aisha is hesitant to get too close to Raj, but she eventually falls in love with him. Raj proposing to Aisha, and she says yes. They have a lavish wedding and a beautiful honeymoon. Dev crashes the wedding and tries to win Aisha back, but she rejects him. Dev leaves, and Aisha and Raj live happily ever after.
