When a young woman is found brutally murdered in a small town, the detective in charge of the case faces many challenges in finding the killer. The victim's family is broken and dysfunctional, and the detective must navigate through their secrets to get to the truth. The investigation leads him to a number of suspects, each with their own dark secrets. As the detective gets closer to the truth, he discovers that the victim was caught up in a web of lies and betrayal, and that everyone involved is hiding something. In the end, the detective must use all his wits and skills to solve the case and bring the killer to justice.
