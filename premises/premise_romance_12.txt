After a whirlwind romance, Mallory and Owen are getting married. However, as the wedding planner begins to put the final touches on their big day, Mallory starts to get cold feet. She begins to worry that she's moving too fast and that she doesn't really know Owen as well as she thought she did.

As the wedding day gets closer, Mallory's anxiety only grows. She confides in her best friend, who helps her to see that she's just going through some normal pre-wedding jitters. With her friend's words of wisdom in mind, Mallory decides to go through with the wedding.

On the big day, everything goes off without a hitch. Mallory and Owen exchange their vows and pledge to love each other for better or for worse. As they walk back down the aisle as husband and wife, they both know that they've made the right decision.
