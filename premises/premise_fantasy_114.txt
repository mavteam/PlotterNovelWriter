The land is in peril. A great evil has been unleashed, and only the bravest of heroes can hope to vanquish it. A group of unlikely friends - a young farm boy, an aged wizard, a skilled archer, and a fearless warrior - come together to embark on a quest to save the kingdom. Along the way, they must face treacherous villains, solve puzzles, and overcome impossible odds. But if they are to succeed, they must first learn to trust one another.
