Romeo and Juliet fall in love and get married against their parents' wishes. Romeo is a poor, uneducated man from a lower-caste, while Juliet is a rich, educated woman from a high-caste. Their parents disown them and they are forced to live in poverty. Despite their hardships, they remain in love and have a child together. As their child grows up, he becomes aware of the differences in his parents' social status and the discrimination they face. He is determined to break down the barriers that divide them and bring his parents back together. He studies hard and gets into a prestigious university. He then uses his education and privileges to help his parents rebuild their lives. In the end, they all live happily ever after.
