In a world of constant war, there is only one way to survive: through strength. The strong take what they want, while the weak cower in the shadows. Xander is a young man who has always dreamed of becoming a warrior. When he finally gets his chance, he quickly realizes that the life of a soldier is not as glamorous as he thought. Bloodshed and death are commonplace, and the only way to survive is to be the strongest. But when Xander meets a beautiful young woman, he starts to question everything he knows. Is there more to life than just being the strongest? Can he really find happiness in a world filled with so much darkness?
