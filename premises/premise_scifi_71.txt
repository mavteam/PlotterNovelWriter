The human race is on the brink of extinction. A new virus has emerged that is highly Contagious and kills its host within days. The few survivors are immune to the virus and are left to try to find a cure. The government has quarantined the survivors in a high-tech facility in the middle of the desert. They are unable to leave and are constantly watched by the government. The survivors must work together to find a cure for the virus before it's too late.
