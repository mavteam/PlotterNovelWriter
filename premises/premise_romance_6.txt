Jennie is a young woman who is struggling to find her place in the world. She's just out of college and working as a waitress to make ends meet. She's been through a lot in her life and she's tired of it. She's ready for a change.

One day, she meets a handsome stranger in the restaurant where she works. His name is Alex and he's clearly not from around here. He's charming and sweet and Jennie can't help but be drawn to him.

They start dating and Jennie is finally happy. She's found someone who loves her for who she is and who makes her feel alive. But then, just as suddenly as he appeared, Alex disappears. Jennie is heartbroken and doesn't know what to do.

But then, one day, she gets a call from him. He's been in a car accident and he's in the hospital. Jennie rushes to be by his side and they're finally able to be together again.
