In a small town in Japan, a young woman is found dead, and the only clue is a mysterious black feather. The police are stumped, and the case goes cold. But the woman's sister, a private detective, is determined to find out what happened to her. She teams up with a local journalist, and together they start to piece together the puzzle. It quickly becomes clear that this is no ordinary murder mystery - there are forces at work that they can't even begin to understand. And the closer they get to the truth, the more dangerous it becomes.
