In a world of constant war and bloodshed, the strong survive and the weak perish. Realms are constantly at war with one another, and entire civilizations have been brought to ruin. In this cruel world, only the strong and the ruthless can survive. One character, a ruthless and powerful warlord, has conquered more realms than anyone else. He is feared and respected by all, and none can stand against him. But even the strongest warlord has enemies, and there are those who plot to overthrow him. As the warlord's enemies close in, he must face challenges from all sides. Betrayals, political maneuvering, and bloody battles await him. In the end, only the warlord can determine his own fate.
