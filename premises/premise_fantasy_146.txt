In a harsh and dark world, a young girl named Annabelle is orphaned and left to fend for herself. She is taken in by a group of bandits who teach her how to fight and survive. Annabelle grows up to be a fierce warrior, and becomes the leader of the bandits. When a rival bandit group attacks, Annabelle leads her group to victory. but at a cost. In the aftermath of the battle, Annabelle is the only one left alive, and she is left with a choice: to live a life of violence, or to find a new purpose.
