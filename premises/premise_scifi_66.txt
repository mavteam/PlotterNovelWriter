While performing a routine mission to check on a newly terraformed planet, a space shuttle and its crew are suddenly pulled off course by a strange space-time anomaly. docking at a nearby space station for help, the crew soon realizes that they are not the only ones affected by the anomaly; the entire space station has been thrown into chaos by the sudden appearance of creatures from another dimension. With the help of the station's AI, the crew must find a way to stop the creatures and repair the space-time anomaly before it destroys the universe.
