In a world of harsh temperatures and great danger, the strong and clever prevent disaster from conquering the land. Half-breed Fiona was born in the worst place possible: on the edge of a frozen tundra. She makes her first steps in a hostile world, learning how to survive in a land where others can barely make it through the day. Valuing strength, speed, and resourcefulness above all else, Fiona must start as the bottom of the pack and work to become the leader of her pack in order to protect them from others who would do them harm.
