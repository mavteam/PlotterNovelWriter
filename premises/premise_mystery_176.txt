In a dark, grungy city filled with crime and corruption, a hard-boiled detective is trying to solve a case that seems impossible. With clues that don't seem to add up and a trail that keeps going cold, the detective is getting frustrated. But just when it seems like there's no hope, a new lead comes in that could finally crack the case wide open. With time running out, the detective race against the clock to put all the pieces together and solve the mystery before it's too late.
