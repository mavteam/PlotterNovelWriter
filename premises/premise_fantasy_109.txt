The kingdom of Amarr is in peril. The evil wizard Darrak has taken control of the king's mind and is using him to wage war on the neighboring kingdom of Zalar. Princess Leilanil is the only one who can stop Darrak, but she's been banished to the forest of Shadows. With the help of her trusty steed, Leilanil makes her way back to the castle and confronts Darrak. After a spectacular battle, she emerges victorious and takes her rightful place as queen.
