Tamara is a successful businesswoman who seems to have it all. She's beautiful, smart, and respected in her field. However, she's been unlucky in love and is starting to feel like she'll never find the right man. When she meets Andrew, she feels like she's finally found her soulmate. He's gorgeous, charming, and he seems to understand her in a way that no one else has. They start dating and Tamara is finally happy. However, just when things seem to be going perfect, Andrew's ex-girlfriend shows up and Tamara starts to wonder if he's really the right man for her.
