Samantha is a young woman living in a future where the world is on the brink of an environmental disaster. The government has been forced to ration resources, and there is little hope for the future. When Samantha is offered a job on a top-secret research team, she jumps at the chance. The team is working on a new form of energy that could save the world. But as they get closer to a breakthrough, they realize that the government is watching them closely. And they will do anything to stop them.
