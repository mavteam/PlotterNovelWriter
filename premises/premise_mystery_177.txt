In a dark and seedy city, a young woman is found dead in an alleyway, her body mutilated beyond recognition. The police are baffled and have no leads. Enter our detective, a hard-boiled private eye with a shady past. He takes the case, against his better judgement, and begins to piece together the victim's last hours. His investigation leads him to a seedy nightclub, where he meets the victim's friend, a beautiful woman with a dark secret. She tells him that the victim was involved with a dangerous man, a man who is now after her. The detective begins to put the pieces together, but the more he learns, the more dangerous the case becomes. With time running out, he must find the killer before the killer finds him.
