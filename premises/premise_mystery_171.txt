After the death of her husband, Jenna moves to a small town to start over. She opens a bookstore and quickly becomes friends with the local librarian, Rachel. One day, Jenna finds a dead body in her store and begins investigating the death with Rachel's help. They quickly discover that the victim was a local woman who was involved in an affair with a married man. Jenna and Rachel's investigation leads them to the victim's husband, who confesses to killing her in a fit of rage.
