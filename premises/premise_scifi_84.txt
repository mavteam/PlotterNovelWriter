In the far future, humanity has colonized the stars and formed a great interstellar empire. However, the empire is in decline, beset by internal strife and external threats. As civilization crumbles, a great war erupts that threatens to destroy all that humanity has built. In the midst of this crisis, a ragtag group of rebels overthrow the corrupt government and set out to rebuild the empire. However, they quickly realize that the task is far greater than they imagined. They must deal with enemies both within and without, as they struggle to bring order to the chaos. Can they succeed? Or will the empire fall once and for all?
