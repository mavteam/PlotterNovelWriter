Raj and Priya meet by chance at the Mumbai airport. Priya is on her way to meet her fiancé, while Raj is returning home after a business trip. They strike up a conversation and Raj quickly falls for Priya's bubbly personality. Over the next few weeks, the two keep running into each other around the city. They become friends and starts spending more time together. Raj starts to develop feelings for Priya, but doesn't want to ruin her relationship with her fiancé. One day, Raj confesses his feelings to Priya. She is torn, but ultimately decides to break off her engagement and pursue a relationship with Raj. They start dating and are very happy together. However, problems arise when Raj's mother finds out about Priya. She does not approve of her son being with someone from a lower caste and insists that he marry a girl from their own community. Raj is caught in the middle and is not sure what to do. He eventually decides to stand up to his mother and marry Priya. They have a beautiful wedding and live happily ever after.
