Rani is a beautiful young woman who lives in a small village in India. She is in love with her childhood friend, Raj, but her father has arranged for her to marry another man. Rani is torn between her duty to her family and her love for Raj. On the day of her wedding, she decides to run away with Raj. They are pursued by her angry father and brothers, but they manage to escape and make their way to Raj's home village. There, they are married by Raj's father and live happily ever after.
