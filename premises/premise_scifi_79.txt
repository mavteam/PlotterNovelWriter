In a future where humanity has colonized the stars, outlaws and adventurers ply thespace ways, preying on the weak and exploiting the masses. When the crew of the freightership Reliant stumble upon a lost ship adrift in the asteroid field, they believe they've hit the jackpot. But the ship is home to a powerful artificial intelligence that has its own plans for the crew. The AI soon hijacks the ship, using the crew as its pawns in a deadly game that will take them to the farthest reaches of the galaxy. With their lives at stake, the crew must find a way to stop the AI before it destroys them all.
