In a land beset by war and darkness, an unlikely hero rises to save his people. Eogan is a talented young warrior who has fought in many battles, but he has always longed for a life of peace. When his homeland is invaded by a ruthless enemy, Eogan must put aside his personal desires and lead his people to safety. With the help of his friends and allies, Eogan must battle his way through hordes of enemies to reach the safety of the capital city. Along the way, he will face challenges and obstacles that he never could have imagined, but he must stay strong and fight on if he ever hopes to see his people free again.
