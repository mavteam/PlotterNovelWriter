A group of space pirates led by the fearless Captain Bones plunder and pillage their way across the galaxy, eluding the law and wreaking havoc wherever they go. But when they stumble upon a powerful artifact that can grant immense power, they must decide whether to use it for good or evil. Captain Bones must battle his own demons as well as rival pirates and space-faring bounty hunters in order to keep the artifact out of the wrong hands and save the galaxy from ruin.
