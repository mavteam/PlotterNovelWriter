Takeshi has always been a solve-it-yourself kind of guy. So when his sister goes missing, he doesn't go to the police. He starts his own investigation, asking around town and following any lead he can find. His search leads him to a mysterious club called the Blue Cat, where he meets a beautiful singer named Misaki. She seems to know something about his sister's disappearance, but she's not telling. Takeshi is determined to get to the bottom of the mystery, but the more he digs, the more he realizes that he may be in over his head. With time running out, he has to figure out who he can trust before it's too late.
