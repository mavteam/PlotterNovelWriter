Paul is a young man living in Paris who is feeling lost and disillusioned with his life. He has just been dumped by his long-term girlfriend and is struggling to find his place in the world. One day, he meets a beautiful woman named Juliette on the subway and is instantly smitten. He starts following her and quickly discovers that she is a struggling actress who is also going through a tough time. The two of them bond over their shared love of art and literature and eventually fall in love. They must overcome many obstacles along the way, including Juliette's ex-boyfriend who is trying to win her back and Paul's own insecurities. In the end, they find happiness in each other and learn to appreciate life more.
