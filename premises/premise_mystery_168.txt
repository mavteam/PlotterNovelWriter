While out walking her dog one early morning, Peggy finds the body of her elderly neighbor Mrs. Patterson in the garden. Peggy is shocked and saddened by the news and decides to take it upon herself to find out who killed Mrs. Patterson. She starts by interviewing the other neighbors and soon learns that Mrs. Patterson was a bit of a gossip. She also evicting a tenant from her rental property who was behind on his rent. Peggy begins to suspect that the tenant, who was last seen arguing with Mrs. Patterson, may be the killer. With the help of her friends and her trusty dog, Peggy does some investigating and eventually discovers that the tenant did indeed kill Mrs. Patterson. She turns him in to the police and justice is served.
