In a dark and seedy city, Nothing is as it seems. The bright lights only serve to highlight the filth and corruption that runs rampant. Our protagonist is a hardboiled private detective, jaded and cynical. He's been hired by a beautiful woman to find her missing sister. The case leads him down a rabbit hole of lies, deceit, and murder. Every person he meets seems to be hiding something, and no one can be trusted. Just when our detective thinks he's got the case solved, a new twist throws everything into chaos. In the end, the truth is revealed, but it may be too late for our hero...
