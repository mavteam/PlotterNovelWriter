When the crops began to wither and the wells ran dry, the people of the kingdom knew that something was wrong. The king sent out a call for adventurers to venture into the forest and find out what was happening. A group of brave heroes took up the call, and ventured deep into the forest. There they found an evil sorceress who had been victimizing the land for her own power. After a long and hard battle, the heroes emerged victorious and freed the kingdom from her tyranny.
