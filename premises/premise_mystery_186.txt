In a seedy back alley of a bustling city, a woman's body is sprawled out on the pavement, a pool of blood pooling around her head. detective Harry Steele is on the case, and he's not going to rest until he finds the killer. With the help of his trusty sidekick, Denton, and the beautiful but mysterious femme fatale, Scarlett, Harry is Hot on the trail of the killer. But with danger lurking around every corner, this is one case that's going to take all of Harry's detective skills to solve.
