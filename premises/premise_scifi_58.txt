In the future, the human race has colonized the solar system. The inner planets are uninhabitable, so the majority of the population lives on Mars. The government is a dictatorship, and the people are struggling to survive. The story follows a group of rebel scientists who are trying to find a way to terraform the inner planets so that they can be inhabitable again. They believe that if they can do this, the government will be forced to step down and the people will be able to live in peace. The rebels are up against overwhelming odds, and they are constantly being pursued by the government. They must use all of their skills and knowledge to stay one step ahead of their pursuers and find a way to save the human race.
