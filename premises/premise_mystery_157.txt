When Detective Sergeant Mike Nash is called to the home of Mildred Worthy, he finds the elderly woman lying dead in her garden. Mrs. Worthy's daughter, Jane, explains that her mother had been receiving threatening letters from an unknown person, and she is convinced that her mother was killed because of them. Detective Nash begins an investigation, and it soon becomes clear that Mrs. Worthy was not the only person in the neighborhood who had been receiving threatening letters. As he speaks to more residents, he discovers that everyone who has received a letter has something in common - they all have. . . . . .a secret that they are keeping from someone. As Nash pieces together the clues, he realizes that he is dealing with a very clever killer who is using people's secrets against them. Can he solve the case before more innocent lives are lost?
