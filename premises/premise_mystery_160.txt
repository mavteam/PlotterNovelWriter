Mrs. Herbert is known for her amazing baking skills. She bakes delicious pies and sells them at the farmer's market. Mrs. Herbert is also known for being a nosy neighbor. She is always asking questions and trying to solve mysteries. When the local librarian is found dead, Mrs. Herbert is determined to find out who did it. She questioning everyone, including the librarian's husband. Ultimately, Mrs. Herbert solves the mystery and brings the killer to justice.
