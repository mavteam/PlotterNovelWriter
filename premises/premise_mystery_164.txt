In this cozy mystery, we follow the story of amateur sleuth Penelope as she tries to solve the murder of her friend, Sheila. Sheila was found dead in her home, and the police are stumped. But Penelope is determined to figure out who did it. With the help of her friend, detective Mike, she starts to piece together the clues. Through a series of twists and turns, they eventually catch the killer. But not before Penelope finds herself in danger.
