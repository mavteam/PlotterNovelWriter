Liz is a librarian who loves nothing more than a good mystery novel. So when she stumbles upon a dead body in the stacks, she's determined to find out who did it. With the help of her cat, Sherlock, and her friend and fellow librarian, John, Liz sets out to solve the mystery. Along the way, they uncover a tangled web of secrets and lies, and not everyone is who they seem. In the end, justice is served and Liz has a new mystery to read.
