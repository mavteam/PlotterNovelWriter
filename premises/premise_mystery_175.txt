In this cozy mystery, the small town of Shepherdstown is rocked by the murder of one of its most prominent citizens. Wealthy businessman Charles Shepherd is found dead in his office, and the police are quick to rule it a homicide. They suspect that Shepherd's business partner, Anna, may have had something to do with his death, but they can't find any evidence to support their theory. Anna is desperate to clear her name, and she enlists the help of her friend, investigative reporter Mallory, to help her find the real killer. Together, they uncover a web of secrets and lies that implicates several members of the community in Shepherd's murder. In the end, the killer is brought to justice, and Shepherdstown can finally rest easy.
