After the loss of her parents, Nala is left to fend for herself in a harsh, wintery world. She makes her way to the nearest town, hopefully to find some help, but instead she's met with distrust and hatred. The townsfolk are cold and ugly, and they make it clear that they don't want her there. Nala is about to give up hope when she meets a kind stranger who offers to help her. Together, they set out on a journey to find a new home for Nala. Along the way, they face danger and hardship, but they also find friendship and love. In the end, they reach their destination, and Nala finally has a place to call home.
