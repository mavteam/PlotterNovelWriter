When local librarian and book club host ML Turner is found dead in her home, the small town of Jasper Falls is in shock. No one can imagine who would want to hurt ML, but as the police begin their investigation, it becomes clear that she was not as well liked as everyone thought. As the townspeople start to point fingers, amateur sleuth Nadine Hunter sets out to find the killer herself. With the help of her book club friends, Nadine starts to piece together a motive for murder. But just as she seems to be getting close to the truth, the killer strikes again. Nadine is in a race against time to solve the murders and catch the killer before anyone else gets hurt. But with everyone in town a suspect, she'll have to tread carefully or she could end up being the killer's next victim.
