Big Jeff is an unlikely hero. A portly space trader, Jeff is more comfortable behind a desk than behind the controls of his ship. But when he's hired to transport a valuable cargo to the other side of the galaxy, he finds himself in the middle of a desperate battle. With his ship damaged and his crew injured, Jeff must risk everything to save his ship and his crew. With the help of his First Mate, Sarah, and his pilot, Spike, Jeff must battle overwhelming odds to make it home safe.
