When Olivia's mom dies, she is left to fend for herself. She spends her days working at the local grocery store and her nights taking care of her younger sister. She doesn't have time for anything else. So when she meets Ian, she is hesitant to let herself get close to him. But Ian is persistent and soon Olivia finds herself falling for him. They start dating and Olivia is finally happy. But then she finds out that Ian is married. She is devastated and ends things with him. But Ian's wife is pregnant and he is planning on leaving her. Olivia is torn. Does she take Ian back and risk getting hurt again? Or does she let him go and live a life of regret?
