In a grimdark world full of war and death, young Ein is an orphan who has only ever known violence. When her parents are killed in a raid, Ein is forced to fend for herself, and she quickly learns that the only way to survive is to be the strongest. She turns to a life of crime, and soon becomes the leader of a small band of thieves. Ein is content to simply live day to day, until she meets a man who tells her about a powerful artifact that can grant any wish. Ein becomes obsessed with finding this artifact, and will stop at nothing to get it. Along the way, she will make allies and enemies, and learn the hard truth that the world is a dark and brutal place.
