In a dark, seedy city filled with crime and corruption, a jaded detective is tasked with solving a brutal murder. The victim is a young woman, and the only clues are a single black rose and a bloody handprint. As the detective delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of lies, betrayal, and destruction that threatens to consume him. With time running out, he must race against the clock to solve the mystery and find the killer before they strike again.
