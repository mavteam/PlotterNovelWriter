In the small town of Shepherdstown, everyone knows everyone. So when local librarian Penelope Bishop is found dead in her home, the whole town is in a panic. Was it murder? Or simply a tragic accident? As the town's police chief, it falls to Jake Shepherd to solve the case. But with few clues and no obvious suspects, he's not sure where to start. Thankfully, Penelope's best friend Nancy is on hand to help Jake with his investigation. Together, they'll uncover all the secrets of Shepherdstown to find out who killed Penelope Bishop.
