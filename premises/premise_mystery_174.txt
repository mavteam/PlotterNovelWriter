The town of Rocksburg is shocked when local librarian and historian, Penelope Primm, is found dead in her home with a rock chess piece clutched in her hand. As the police begin their investigation, they quickly realize that they're dealing with a murder. Detective Sergeant Jill Jackson is put in charge of the case and she immediately begins to put the pieces together. She interviews Penelope's family and friends, and it quickly becomes clear that someone in the close-knit community had a motive to kill her. As Jill gets closer to uncovering the killer's identity, she finds herself in danger. With the help of her trusty sidekick, William the cat, she's able to put together the clues and solve the case before she becomes the next victim.
