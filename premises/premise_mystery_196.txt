A young woman named Akiko has just been released from prison after serving time for a crime she didn't commit. She is eager to start over, but quickly realizes that the world outside is not as forgiving as she thought it would be. Desperate and alone, she takes a job as a private investigator in a seedy part of town. It isn't long before she discovers that her new job is anything but simple, and that the city is full of dark secrets. As Akiko delves deeper into the mysterious underworld of the city, she soon realizes that she may be in over her head. With danger lurking around every corner, can she solve the cases and clear her name?
