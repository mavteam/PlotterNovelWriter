Lillian Harper lives a tranquil life running her bed and breakfast in the small town of Shepherdstown. But when one of her guests is found murdered in her own backyard, Lillian becomes the prime suspect in the case. With the help of her best friend, Peggy, and the investigative skills she picked up from her late mother, Lillian sets out to clear her name and find the real killer. Along the way, she uncovers dark secrets, old grudges, and a web of deceit that threatens to destroy her idyllic life in Shepherdstown.
