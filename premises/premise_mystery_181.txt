Irene throws a party to celebrate her move to a new apartment complex. Little did she know that one of her new neighbors, a man named Vincent, would be brutally murdered that night. The police rule it as a robbery gone wrong, but Irene can't help but feel like there's more to the story. Irene begins to look into the case on her own, and quickly discovers that Vincent was involved in some shady business deals. He was also being blackmailed by someone, and it seems that his death was no accident. As Irene gets closer to the truth, she finds herself in danger. Can she solve the mystery before it's too late?
