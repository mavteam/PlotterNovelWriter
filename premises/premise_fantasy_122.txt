The high fantasy story follows the journey of a young woman named Sarah who is taken captive by a group of bandits. She is held in a remote cabin in the woods and is repeatedly raped and tortured by her captors. When they are finally caught by the authorities, Sarah is the only one who survives. She is taken in by a kindly old woman who helps her heal both physically and emotionally. With her newfound strength, Sarah sets out to find the bandits and make them pay for what they did to her. She eventually finds them and kills them all, but not before they take her on one last brutal journey.
