When Rachel and Alex meet by chance at a local coffee shop, they instantly hit it off. However, both are in relationships with other people. They become friends, confiding in each other about their relationships and giving each other advice. Over time, they realize they have feelings for each other and eventually confess. They break up with their significant others and start dating. They face challenges along the way, but their love for each other prevails. They get married and live happily ever after.
