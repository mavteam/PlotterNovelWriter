Lydia Gold is a librarian who spends her days shelving books and her evenings reading them. She lives a relatively quiet life until one day when she stumbling upon a dead body in the library. Lydia is determined to figure out who killed the victim and why. She enlists the help of her best friend and fellow librarian, Julia, and together they set out to solve the mystery. They interview suspects, comb through clues, and eventually piece together the killer's motive. In the end, justice is served and Lydia can go back to her quiet life, albeit with a new appreciation for solving puzzles.
