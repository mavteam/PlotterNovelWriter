Far in the land of Dorin there lived a great kingdom. The people were thriving and happy under the leadership of King Gelidor. One day, however, a dark force from the north attacked. The people were forced to flee, and the kingdom was destroyed. Years later, a group of adventurers sets out to find the lost kingdom. Along the way they battle monsters, make new friends, and uncover long-forgotten secrets. Finally, they reach the lost kingdom, where they must face the enemy that destroyed it. With courage and strength, they triumph and Dorin is restored to its former glory.
