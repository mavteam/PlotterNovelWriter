In the year 3019, humanity has finally made contact with intelligent alien life. The first few years are spent learning about each other and establishing trade routes. But soon, tensions start to rise. The aliens are xenophobic and hardware-hungry, and they begin to strip-mine Earth's resources. They also starts kidnapping humans for unknown reasons. With the help of some friendly aliens, a group of humans build a massive space ship and begin to fight back. They wage a bloody war against the aliens, destroying their shipyards and winning back control of Earth. In the end, the humans are victorious, but at a great cost.
