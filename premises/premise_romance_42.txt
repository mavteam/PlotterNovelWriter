When Aisha meets Raj at a wedding, she knows it is meant to be. Raj is a kind and gentle man, and Aisha can't help but be drawn to him. They begin dating, and despite the fact that they are from different cultures, they are happy together. But when Aisha's parents find out, they are not pleased. They want her to date someone from their own community, someone who is wealthy and connected. They give her an ultimatum: date the man they have chosen for her, or they will disown her. Aisha is torn, but she knows what she wants. She decides to follow her heart and stay with Raj. This decision comes with consequences, however, and Aisha finds herself cut off from her family and friends. But she knows that she made the right choice, and she and Raj are happier than ever.
