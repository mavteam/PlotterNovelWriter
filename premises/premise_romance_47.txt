After the loss of her parents, Shannon is left reeling. She moves to her grandfather's house in the hopes of finding some peace, but instead discovers a whole other world. Shannon discovers that she is a witch and that her grandfather is a Warlock. Together they must navigate the world of the supernatural and find a way to stop the evil forces that are trying to destroy them. Along the way, Shannon falls in love with a dangerous vampire and must choose between the life she knows and the one she is destined for.
