The year is 4302. The Milky Way is in the throes of a great war. The fate of the galaxy hangs in the balance. The great war began as a conflict between the Galactic Republic and the Separatist Alliance. However, it quickly escalated into a full-blown war that engulfed the entire galaxy. Now, after years of fighting, the Republic is on the verge of defeat. The Separatists have gained the upper hand, and they are poised to deliver the final blow. However, there is still hope. A small band of brave heroes has embarked on a daring mission to stop the Separatists and save the galaxy. These heroes will face overwhelming odds, but they cannot fail. The future of the galaxy depends on them.
