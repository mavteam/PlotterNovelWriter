Sabrina is a young woman who has always been skeptical of the paranormal, until she meets a handsome man named Luke at a party. Luke is a vampire, and he is instantly drawn to Sabrina. They begin a passionate affair, but Sabrina is unaware of the dangers that come with loving a vampire. As their relationship deepens, Luke begins to show Sabrina the dark side of his world. She is horrified by the violence and bloodshed, but she cannot deny her feelings for Luke. Eventually, she must decide whether to embrace Luke's world or walk away from him forever.
