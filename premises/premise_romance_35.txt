When Mallika meets Rahul at a wedding, she can tell that he is the one for her. She starts to pursue him, but he is hesitant because of her status as a lower-caste woman. After much convincing, Rahul finally agrees to date her and they fall madly in love with each other. However, they keep their relationship a secret from Mallika's family because they know they would never approve. One day, while Mallika is at work, her family finds out about her relationship and they drag her home. They tell her that she must end things with Rahul immediately and marry the man they have chosen for her. Mallika knows she can't live without Rahul, so she runs away with him. They get married and start a new life together, but it is not easy. They face discrimination and challenges because of their different background, but they are determined to make it work.
