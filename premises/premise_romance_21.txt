When Antoinette Gautier meetsbrooding artist, Claude Monet, she is immediately drawn to his dark aura. However, she soon discovers that beneath his gruff exterior lies a kind and gentle soul. As the two begin to fall in love, they must overcome the obstacles of their different social classes and the disapproval of Antoinette's father. But, despite all of this, their love is true and will endure the test of time.
