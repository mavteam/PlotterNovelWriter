When the Earth is invaded by a ruthless alien race, a small group of humans manage to escape in a recruiting starship. They find themselves stranded on a hostile planet, and must band together to find a way back to Earth. Along the way, they make new allies and enemies, and come to realize that the invaders are not as invincible as they first seemed. With the help of their new friends, they launch a daring attack on the enemy's home base, and finally liberate Earth.
