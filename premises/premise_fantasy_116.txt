Ramza Beoulve is the youngest son of a noble house in the nation of Gallione. When Ramza's father and two brothers are killed in a political plot, Ramza is forced to flee the city with his friend Delita Heiral. The two men join the mercenary group, the Ghosts of Val grace, and quickly find themselves in the middle of a great conflict. The kingdom of Ivalice is in upheaval, as the forces of the Lion War seek to topple the ruling government. Ramza and Delita find themselves caught up in the conflict, and soon discover that the war is being manipulated by a greater power. As they fight to uncover the truth, the two men must face their own demons, and decide what is truly worth fighting for.
