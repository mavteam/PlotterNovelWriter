Camille is a young woman living in Paris who is desperately searching for love. She has been in and out of relationships, but has never been able to find that special someone. She is beginning to lose hope until she meets Olivier, a charming and handsome man who sweeps her off her feet. The two begin a whirlwind romance and everything seems perfect. However, Olivier is hiding a dark secret and when Camille finds out, their whole world comes crashing down.
