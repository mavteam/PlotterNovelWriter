In a noir anime mystery story, a cynical detective named Inspector Jakurai is assigned to solve a string of murders that have been turning up around the city. With the help of his assistant, a talented young woman named Detective Kiyomi, Inspector Jakurai must use all of his wit and cunning to track down the killer and bring them to justice. The story features a complicated web of lies, deceit, and betrayal, as Inspector Jakurai slowly begins to unravel the truth behind the murders. With time running out, he must put all of the pieces together before the killer can strike again. This noir anime mystery story is full of suspense, twists, and turns that will keep you guessing until the very end. With its complex characters and gripping plot, this is one mystery story that you won't want to miss.
