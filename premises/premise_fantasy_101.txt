The kingdom of Alabaster is in peril. The king has been assassinated and the queen has been kidnapped. The kingdom is in chaos. The prince, who was away at the time of the attack, must now return home and take up the mantle of king. He must find his mother and rescue her from the clutches of the evil wizard who is behind the coup. Along the way, the prince will meet friends and enemies, and discover the truth about his parentage. He will have to fight for his life and the future of the kingdom.
